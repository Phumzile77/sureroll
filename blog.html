Payroll Mistakes Small Businesses Make

Read More

Small businesses often face several common payroll challenges that can lead to compliance issues and financial risks.

One major issue is the incorrect classification of employees, where workers are mistakenly treated as independent contractors instead of employees.

In addition, missing payroll deadlines, such as late salary payments or delayed tax submissions, can result in penalties and legal complications.

Poor record keeping is another concern, as inadequate payroll records may cause disputes and errors in salary calculations.

Furthermore, incorrect tax deductions, particularly in PAYE or UIF, can lead to financial losses or fines.

Lastly, failing to regularly update payroll information when employee details change can result in inaccurate payroll processing and further administrative problems.
